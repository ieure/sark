WTF IS A TLA?

Acronyms. Abbreviations. Call them what you will, they're still a PITA*.

They're fine if you know what they mean - and let's face it, nobody wants to keep typing out Creature From The Black Lagoon - but for those times when you don't know, here is the definitive guide for all the games from about 1990 to present, plus a few other relevant games, some technical terms and other stuff you might find useful.

*it's in the list!
